PRJ-001 · 2024

HUL Business Valuation

Business Valuation

Intrinsic valuation of Hindustan Unilever Limited (NSE: HINDUNILVR) built entirely on a Discounted Cash Flow framework. The Excel model forecasts free cash flows to the firm (FCFF) over an explicit projection period, estimates the terminal value, and discounts back at a WACC computed via CAPM. Key assumptions — revenue growth, EBIT margins, capex intensity, and the cost of equity — are stress-tested through a sensitivity table to arrive at a fair value range for the stock.

Deliverable
Excel Workbook (.xlsx)
Methods
DCF / FCFF WACC / CAPM Gordon Growth Model Sensitivity Analysis

PRJ-002 · 2024

Comparative Study of ETF Volatility Prediction between LSTM and ARIMA

Quantitative Finance

A comparative study evaluating the predictive accuracy of Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks against the classical ARIMA model for ETF volatility forecasting for S&P 500. Implemented in Python on Google Colab, the project covers time-series preprocessing, LSTM architecture design with dropout regularization, ARIMA model fitting, and a head-to-head evaluation of both models on out-of-sample data. Supplemented by an Excel workbook with supporting data analysis and a full written report documenting methodology and results.
